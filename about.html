Trove Wealth is a UK personal finance app made by a small independent team. No bank logins. No ads. Just a clear picture of your financial life.

Why we built Trove

Trove started from a personal frustration — existing finance apps either required you to hand over bank credentials, were built for the US market, or buried the useful features behind a complex interface. We wanted something simple: one place to see your net worth, track spending, manage ISAs and pensions, and get plain-English answers about your money without needing a financial adviser. So we built it.

We're a small team based in the UK, building tools we'd want to use ourselves. Trove Wealth is our iOS app, and it's the first product from Trovetech Ltd — a company incorporated in England & Wales (company number available on request).

What we stand for

Private by design

We don't connect to your bank. We don't sell your data. Your financial information lives in your account and nowhere else.

Honest and transparent

No dark patterns, no hidden fees. Free means free. Pro features are clearly labelled and fairly priced.

Built for the UK

ISAs, pensions, tax years, National Insurance — we speak UK finance natively. Not a US app with a £ sign bolted on.

What Trove Wealth does

Trove Wealth lets you manually track all your accounts — current accounts, savings, ISAs, pensions, investments, property, and debts — in one place. You can set budgets, track recurring bills, monitor your financial health score, and ask an AI assistant questions about your money in plain English. It's free to download, with a Pro tier for advanced features.
